Amsterdam

Amsterdam's history dates back to the 13th century, and remnants of its past are beautifully preserved in the city's architecture and landmarks. Wander through the historic neighborhoods, where 17th-century canal houses stand proudly, showcasing the city's Golden Age heritage. Visit the Anne Frank House, a poignant reminder of wartime history, and explore the centuries-old churches and museums that dot the cityscape.
